\subsection*{Math spacing}
	
Normal space in math mode:

\[1 2\]

Custom spaces in math mode::

% [mu] is the math unit equal to 1/18 em, where em is taken from the math symbols family, according to ShareLaTeX.
\[1\!2\] % -3/18 of \quad (= -3 mu)
\[1\,2\] % 3/18 of \quad (= 3 mu)
\[1\:2\] % 4/18 of \quad (= 4 mu)
\[1\;2\] % 5/18 of \quad (= 5 mu)
\[1\ 2\] % equivalent of space in normal text
\[1\quad2\] % space equal to the current font size (= 18 mu)
\[1\qquad2\] % twice of \quad (= 36 mu)

\subsection*{Phantom spacing}

The command \emph{phantom} creates a horizontal space according the width of the argument.

\[
	\begin{pmatrix}
		123 			& 456\\
		\phantom{12}3	& 4\phantom{56}
	\end{pmatrix}
\]
